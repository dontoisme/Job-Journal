✉ Email Integration & Lifecycle

› Patterns match: greenhouse lever ashby smartrecruiters workday icims
› Classify via RESOLUTION_SIGNALS keyword dict: confirmation vs. resolution
› Pair to application record: one confirmation + one resolution per app in application_emails
› Auto-transitions: rejection → status="rejected", interview → status="interview"
› Ghost detection: confirmed >14 days with no resolution → pairing_status="ghosted"
